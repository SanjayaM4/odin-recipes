Basmati Rice

Description

Basmati, is a variety of long, slender-grained aromatic rice which is traditionally grown in India, Pakistan, and Nepal. As of 2019, India accounted for 65% of the international trade in basmati rice, while Pakistan accounted for the remaining 35%.

Simple basmati rice, like in the restaurants!

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups water
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • ¼ cup frozen green peas
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

Directions

  1. In a saucepan bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. When rice is cooked, stir in peas and cumin. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
